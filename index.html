Classification model to red wine quality

Classify products can provide valuable information to producers when defining marketing and pricing strategies. In this context, this work aims to classify red wines into categories of "good" and "bad" quality (or superior and inferior, as preferred). This classification allows producers to tailor their marketing and pricing approaches more effectively, targeting the right consumer segments and positioning their products in the market in a way that reflects their quality. Such a classification not only aids in optimizing sales strategies but also helps in enhancing customer satisfaction by aligning product offerings with consumer expectations and preferences.

Spatial Analysis of Tuberculosis Incidence in the Municipalities of Rio de Janeiro

Tuberculosis is an infectious disease that continues to concern health professionals and public health managers. The purpose of this work is to use Exploratory Spatial Data Analysis methods to measure the spatial relationship of tuberculosis incidence in the municipalities of the State of Rio de Janeiro. The results indicate a strong relationship of high incidences in the state capital and its surroundings. Considering the problem and the results, it is suggested that a public policy that starts from the state capital and spreads to the neighboring municipalities would be more efficient.

Brawlhalla Recommendation System

The aim is to help users select characters that best fit their preferred playstyle or narrative role, taking into account factors such as strength, dexterity, speed, defense, and the types of weapons they wield. The system analyzes the character data and provides suggestions that align with the user's preferences or the requirements of a specific game scenario or story development..
